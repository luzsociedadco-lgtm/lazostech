# Platform Workflows

LazosTech combines governance processes with verifiable activity execution and environmental tracking.

This document describes the main operational workflows within the platform.

---

# Governance Workflow

Governance decisions follow a structured lifecycle designed to ensure transparency and accountability.

```
Assembly
   ↓
Create Resolution
   ↓
Deliberation
   ↓
Voting
   ↓
Assign Responsible
   ↓
Execute Activity
   ↓
Verification
   ↓
Completion
   ↓
Reward Redemption
```

Key roles involved:

* Assembly members
* Responsible participant
* Verifier

Only after verification can rewards be redeemed.

---

# Recycling Workflow

The recycling workflow allows participants to register environmental contributions and receive rewards once the activity is verified.

```
User performs recycling activity
   ↓
Recycling record is submitted
   ↓
Verification of activity
   ↓
Reward issued in NUDOS tokens
```

This workflow allows institutions to track environmental impact while encouraging sustainable behavior.

---

# Mission Execution Workflow

Governance assemblies may create resolutions that assign missions to participants.

Example missions may include:

* organizing sustainability events
* coordinating recycling programs
* conducting community initiatives
* participating in institutional projects

Mission workflow:

```
Resolution created
   ↓
Responsible participant assigned
   ↓
Mission execution
   ↓
Verification by authorized participant
   ↓
Reward redemption
```

---

# Reward Redemption Workflow

Once a resolution or activity has been verified, the responsible participant may redeem the corresponding reward.

```
Activity verified
   ↓
Responsible participant claims reward
   ↓
NUDOS tokens issued
```

This ensures that rewards are tied to completed and verified contributions.

---

# Transparency

All workflows are recorded on-chain, including:

* governance resolutions
* activity verification
* reward distribution
* environmental contributions

This creates a permanent and verifiable record of institutional activities.

---

# Future Workflow Extensions

Future development may introduce additional workflows such as:

* cross-institutional initiatives
* sustainability reporting
* automated verification mechanisms

These workflows will continue to expand the platform's ability to support transparent and accountable initiatives.
