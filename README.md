# LazosTech

Blockchain infrastructure for transparent governance and social impact programs.

---

## Overview

LazosTech is an open-source blockchain platform designed to improve transparency, trust, and accountability in institutional governance and social initiatives.

The platform enables organizations, universities, and communities to record decisions and governance processes on-chain, ensuring verifiable and tamper-proof records.

---

## Problem

Many institutions and social programs lack transparent mechanisms for verifying decisions and governance processes.

Centralized systems can be altered, records can be lost, and stakeholders may have limited access to trustworthy information.

---

## Solution

LazosTech provides a decentralized governance engine built on blockchain infrastructure.

Key actions such as proposals, voting, and final resolutions are recorded on-chain, creating a permanent and publicly verifiable audit trail.

---

## Social Impact

The platform aims to strengthen transparency and accountability in:

- universities
- NGOs
- social impact initiatives
- institutional governance systems

This helps build trust and improve oversight in environments where transparency is essential.

---

## Technical Architecture

LazosTech is built using a modular smart contract architecture.

Core components:

- Diamond Standard (EIP-2535)
- Solidity smart contracts
- Foundry development framework
- Base blockchain network
- Frontend dashboard for governance visualization
